# User Profiles

- **Daily**: Users who purchase every day.
- **Weekly**: Users who purchase once a week.
- **Monthly**: Users who purchase once a month.
- **Quarterly**: Users who purchase every three months.
- **Yearly**: Users who purchase once a year.
